iStore Account and Data Deletion

This page explains how iStore users can request deletion of account-related and synced store-profile data.

iStore is operated by AMZ Solutions. If you want to request deletion of your account-related data or synced store-profile data, follow the steps below.

To request deletion, email amz.solution.05@gmail.com with the subject line: iStore Data Deletion Request

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to amz.solution.05@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: iStore Data Deletion Request.
  3. Include your store name and owner/contact name used during app setup.
  4. Include the contact phone number or email address associated with the store profile, if available.
  5. State whether you want:
    • deletion of synced store-profile data only, or
    • full account-related data deletion associated with the synced profile record.

What Data May Be Deleted

Upon a valid deletion request, we may delete the synced store-profile data associated with your iStore profile, such as:

  • store name
  • nature of business
  • owner or contact name
  • owner phone number
  • owner email address
  • app version
  • platform
  • device type
  • operating system version
  • profile creation and update timestamps

What Data May Be Kept

The following may be retained where reasonably necessary:

  • records required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or compliance purposes
  • limited support or audit records related to the deletion request
  • crash or technical logs that cannot reasonably be linked back to a specific store owner identity

Local Device Data

Most business records in iStore are stored locally on your device unless explicitly synced or exported. Local data such as products, orders, customers, inventory, expenses, and receipts can usually be removed by:

  • clearing app data on the device
  • uninstalling the app
  • deleting or overwriting local backups and exported files you created

Retention Period

We will aim to process valid deletion requests within a reasonable period. Some limited records may be retained for up to 90 days where needed for operational, legal, fraud-prevention, security, or support purposes.
